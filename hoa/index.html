Common Issues
What our legal team finds most often
01

Vendor Contracts Without Competitive Bidding

Boards awarding contracts to favored vendors without obtaining competitive bids as required by statute or governing documents — often involving six-figure annual contracts.

02

Legal Fees That Benefit the Board

Associations spending six figures on legal fees and management costs that primarily benefit board members and management companies — not the owners who pay assessments.

03

Election Irregularities

Improper notice, manipulated proxies, election violations, and board seats that were never properly contested — allowing the same directors to maintain control indefinitely.

04

Reserve Fund Deficiencies

Inadequate reserve funding leading to surprise special assessments — often after years of board decisions to waive reserve contributions or fund capital expenditures improperly.
